    • SELECTION PROCESS

    Selection Procedure:
    The company conducts recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 4 rounds. These rounds are as follows:

  • Written Exam

  • Group discussion

  • Technical Interview

  • HR Interview

    • Academic Criteria:
  • Minimum 60 percent throughout 10th,12th and graduation.

  • No backlogs should be there.

    • Pattern of Written Exam:
    The TCS test pattern is divided into the following sections: 
            
                        TESTING AREAS                       NUMBER OF QUESTIONS
                        
                        Aptitude                                 40
                        
                        Technical                                70
                        
                        Total                                   110
    The total time allotted to the written exam is 115 minutes. There is 1/3rd negative marking of in the paper. 
                            
  • Aptitude section consists of questions based on Blood Relation, Time & Distance, Conditions and Assumptions and logical questions.
                        
  • Technical section consists of questions from core subjects such as C, C++, DBMS, Computer Organization and Operating System.
    • Overall the level of the paper is moderate. Only those candidates who clear the written exam will qualify for the next round.
