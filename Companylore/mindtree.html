    Selection Procedure:
    The company conducts recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 3 rounds. These rounds are as follows:

  • Written Exam

  • Technical Interview

  • HR Interview

    • Academic Criteria:
  • Candidate must be a B.tech,BCA,MCA,BE student.

  • Education gap of not more than one year.

    The TCS test pattern is divided into the following sections: 
                            TESTING AREAS                       NUMBER OF QUESTIONS

    The total time allotted to the written exam is 85 minutes that is 20 minutes for the aptitude section and 20 minutes for technical section and 10 minutes is given for writing an essay. 
                            
  • Aptitude section consists of  questions from the topics like algebra, time & work, time, speed & distance, arithmetic, percentages, profit & loss, geometry, etc. This section is of moderate level.
                        
  • Verbal section consists Basic Questions from english language, like synonyms,antonyms,sentence completion and correction etc 
  •  Reasoning  section consists of reasoning based questions from common Topics of Reasoning skills
  • Technical section questins are from the topics like C language, OS, Computer Organization, Networks, etc along with usually 2 questions from coding , wherein the student is required to code in any language of thier choice
                        
                        Overall the level of the paper is easy. Only those candidates who clear the written exam will qualify for the next round.
