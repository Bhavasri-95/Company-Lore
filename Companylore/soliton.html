ABOUT US

Soliton is a technology company that offers research, development and consulting services to solve challenging problems in the areas of (1) Measurement and Automation and (2) Machine Vision. Soliton has a highly talented and skilled multidisciplinary team of engineers with expertise in software and hardware, and a track record of providing solutions for challenging technical problems. Soliton has won 23 international awards for its innovative solutions to date.
