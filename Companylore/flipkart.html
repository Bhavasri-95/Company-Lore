ABOUT US

Flipkart is an e-commerce company founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. The company is registered in Singapore, but has its headquarters in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.[4][5][6] Flipkart has launched its own product range under the name "DigiFlip" with products including tablets, USBs, and laptop bags. In May 2014, Flipkart received $210 million from DST Global, in July 2014 it raised $1 billion led by existing investors Tiger Global and South Africa's media group Naspers and in May 2015 it raised $550 million from some of its existing investors. Flipkart's last fundraising round in May 2015 had pegged its valuation at $15 billion.[10]In February 2016, Morgan Stanley, marked down its investment value to $11 billion.
